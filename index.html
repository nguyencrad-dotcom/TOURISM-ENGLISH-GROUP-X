★★★★★

SPECIAL-PURPOSE VEHICLE

Glass-bottom boat

For those who wish to marvel at the vibrant marine life without getting wet, the glass-bottom boat is a perfect choice, especially in the pristine waters of Vinh Hy Bay and Nha Trang Bay . This specialized vessel allows families and elderly travelers to clearly observe coral reefs and colorful schools of fish through a transparent floor while cruising the "World’s Most Beautiful Bays"

MUST TRY

Cyclos

To truly soak in the atmosphere of the coastal city of Nha Trang, nothing beats a slow-paced cyclo tour along the iconic Tran Phu beach road . These traditional three-wheeled bicycles offer a nostalgic and romantic way to enjoy the sea breeze and sightsee the city's highlights at night, providing a peaceful contrast to the bustling "24h entertainment city" vibe

MUST TRY

Terrain Vehicles

In the "Little Desert" areas like Mui Dinh or Tanyoli, thrill-seekers can conquer the massive shifting sand dunes using powerful UTV terrain vehicles . This adventurous mode of transport offers an exhilarating experience as you navigate the wind-swept golden sands, highlighting the unique "Sea - Forest - Desert" ecosystem that sets the new Khanh Hoa apart
